ABOUT THE GAMES

Sidewalk Doggo and Polar Snakes

We created environmental awareness games to encourage people to learn about the health of the environment in a fun way. Sidewalk Doggo is a pong style game in which the player needs to prevent the dog from touching the hot sidewalk. Polar Snakes is a snake style game in which the player needs to collect food while ice quickly freezes over.
