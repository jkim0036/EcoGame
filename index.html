ABOUT THE ENVIRONMENT

Environmental Damage

As the human population continues to grow, we pose a huge risk to the health of our environment. Whether it is the overpopulation of the Earth, the need to clear the land for construction, or the poisonous emissions from our automobiles, humans are actively contributing to the degredation of the earth.

Degrading Glaciers


ECO wants to bring awareness to the growing environmental problem in an informative but fun way. We hope to reach a wide audience through our interactive games as well as our information pages located in our menu bar. Humans have always had an impact on the Earth; however, over the past couple decades, our negative impact on the environment has been increasing at an alarming rate. We hope to show the importance and value in preserving our environment's health.
